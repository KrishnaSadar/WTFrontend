Our Services

Following are the services that AgriConnect market provides for Farmers:

1. Post Ads

A farmer creates a profile, advertisements are posted for agricultural equipment to rent for off-seasons.

2. Browse and Filter

Explore varieties of farming equipment, also filter on basis of various parameters.

3. Check Availabilities

Check the available time and slots for the required equipment in order to book them.

4. Book for Certain Timeline

Customers create a profile and book farming equipment for their own uses and benefits.

5. Connect through Chats

Owners and customers connect through chats and can send voice messages to make deals.

6. Notifications on Updates

Get notified through SMS for booking and other related information & updates.
